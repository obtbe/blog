Mathematical Problems

1.
Evaluate \(\displaystyle \int \left( x^7 \log(x^2) + \log(x^2) e^x \right) dx\).
2.
Find the derivative of \((\cos x)^{\sin x}\).
3.
Compute \(\displaystyle \int_{-\pi/2}^{\pi/2} e^x \cos x \, dx\).
4.
Find \(\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\tanh\left(\frac{x^2}{2}\right)}{\left(\sin\left(\frac{x}{2}\right)\right)^2}\) where \(\tanh x = \frac{e^x - e^{-x}}{e^x + e^{-x}}\).
5.
Compute the distance from point \(\mathbf{x} = (2, 0, 1, -3)\) to the plane \(3x_1 - 2x_2 + 2x_3 - 4x_4 = 1\).
6.
Let \(A, B, C\) be \(n \times n\) invertible matrices. Simplify \(C^{-1}(A^2B^{-1})^{-2}A^{-2}(CA^{-3})^{-2}(CB^{-1})^2\).
7.
Let \(A = \begin{pmatrix} 4 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 2 & 3 \\ 1 & 3 & 2 \end{pmatrix}\). Find the maximal eigenvalue of \(A^3\).
8.
Let \(A \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times n}\) be a full rank matrix. If you are allowed to modify any \(m < n\) entries of \(A\), what is the achievable lower-bound on the rank of the modified matrix?
9.
Let \(X_i\) be i.i.d. uniform random variables on \([0,1]\). Find the mean value of \(Y = \max\{X_1, X_2, \dots, X_{2025}\}\).
10.
A fair coin is tossed repeatedly. Find \(E[l(HH)]\) and \(E[l(HT)]\), where \(l(X)\) is the number of tosses until the first appearance of pattern \(X\).
11.
A traveler starts in city A. Each day: in A, stays with probability 1/3, goes to B or C with probability 1/3 each; in B, goes to A or C with probability 1/2 each. Find the expected number of days to get from A to C.
12.
Find the dimension of the space of polynomials of 4 variables with degree not greater than 3.
13.
In a graph with \(n = 10\) nodes, each pair is connected with probability \(p = 1/4\). Find the expected number of triangles.
14.
Let \(\mathbf{v} = (v_1, \dots, v_n)\) where \(v_i \in \{0,1,\dots,q-1\}\).
(a) Total number of such vectors
(b) Number differing from \(\mathbf{0}\) in exactly \(t\) positions
(c) Number differing from \(\mathbf{0}\) in at most \(t\) positions
15.
Given \(a_{n+1} = \frac{a_n}{1 + n a_n}\) with \(a_1 = 1\), find \(a_n\) in closed form.
16.
What will be the output of: 
func = lambda a, b: a if b == 0 else func(b, a%b)
a = 144
b = 336
print(func(a,b))
